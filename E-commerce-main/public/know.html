Interesting Facts About Yoga

  • Yoga is an ancient practice that originated in India over 5,000 years ago.
  • The word "yoga" comes from Sanskrit and means "union" or "connection."
  • There are various styles of yoga, including Hatha Yoga, Vinyasa Yoga, Iyengar Yoga, Ashtanga Yoga, and more.
  • Practicing yoga can improve flexibility, strength, and balance.
  • Yoga is not just about physical postures; it also includes breath control, meditation, and ethical guidelines.
  • International Yoga Day is celebrated on June 21st each year.
  • Yoga has been shown to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression.
