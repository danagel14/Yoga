SOULMAT FAQ

What is the return policy?

You can return any unopened product within 30 days for a full refund. For more details, please check our return policy page.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping fees and delivery times vary based on the destination.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.

What materials are used in your yoga mats?

Our yoga mats are made from high-quality, eco-friendly materials such as natural rubber, TPE, and PVC-free materials.

Can I use a discount code on sale items?

Discount codes cannot be used on items that are already on sale. Sale prices are final and cannot be combined with other promotions.
