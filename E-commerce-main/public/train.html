Recommended Yoga Exercises

  • Mountain Pose (Tadasana): A foundational standing pose that helps improve posture and balance.
  • Downward Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana): A full-body stretch that strengthens the arms, shoulders, and legs.
  • Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I): Strengthens the legs and arms, and opens the chest and shoulders.
  • Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II): Builds strength in the legs and core, and improves concentration.
  • Child's Pose (Balasana): A restful pose that stretches the back and hips, and promotes relaxation.
  • Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana): Strengthens the back, glutes, and legs, and opens the chest and shoulders.
  • Corpse Pose (Savasana): A relaxation pose often practiced at the end of a yoga session to promote mental calmness and physical relaxation.
